Fever LTD · Homepage concepts · June 2026

Three directions for the homepage.

Each is an independent, static prototype built on the same real artists and releases — so the comparison is about look and feel, not content. Open any one full-screen, then we narrow to a direction.

A — Editorial White

Gallery

Bright, clean, white + red. A full-screen rotating artist hero, Sunday-Drive style. Bricolage Grotesque + Fraunces. Sharp corners, hairline rules, lots of air. The photos are the only color.

View concept A
 B — Dark Moody

After Midnight

Near-black, photographic, glowing. A restrained, contained artist carousel with a mono index, now under a broadsheet-style masthead (topline → big logo → ruled nav). Space Grotesk + Spectral + Space Mono. Red as a signal light. Switcher toggles a light scheme too. Premium-underground.

View concept B
 C — Broadsheet Bold

Front Page

High-contrast print energy. An editorial split/masthead hero with ruled grid. Archivo (black) + DM Serif Display. Black + red with an acid spot highlight. Loud but disciplined.

View concept C
 D — Pressroom

Gallery Press

Warm paper, ink & red — light, but with teeth. Borrows B's masthead and carousel, reworked light and artsy-punk: all-caps condensed headings (the Fever store cue), a fill+outline word mix with a red period, baseline rules and a mono ticker. Museum-placard release cards. Big Shoulders + Fraunces, with a Syne/Gallery option; scheme flips to dark.
