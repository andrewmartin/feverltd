# The Chain Gang of 1974

- **Artist:** Kamtin Mohager (solo project) — founder of the Fever LTD label
- **Genre:** Electronic / synth-rock / indie
- **Fever LTD releases:** _Honey Moon Drips_ — Vinyl (signed + unsigned editions); _Besides/Pollen_ — Vinyl
- **Notes:** Kamtin Mohager is the Fever LTD client/founder, so all photos here are of him. Genuine press photos are scarce online (most are paywalled on Getty); the live shots below are CC-licensed via Wikimedia Commons.
