- **Origin:** Montana
- **Genre:** Post-hardcore / screamo
- **Members:** Gunnar Stephan, Trevin Baker, Caleb Haynes, Jax Sutton
- **Fever LTD release:** _Ghosts_ — CD (EP, May 2024)
- **Notes:** This is the band, NOT the Jason Aldean country song. Confirmed via IDIOTEQ, Rock'N'Load, Distorted Sound, Bandcamp, and Fever LTD Big Cartel.
